Instructional effectiveness is further extended when students transfer their learning to the home environment. As Annenberg Learner (n.d.) explains on page 26, "understandings are again reinforced when shared with family members" (p. 26). By sending home physical artifacts, such as worksheets or observation journals, teachers invite families to participate in the scientific process. Ramlal (2023) explains "vocabulary relates to understanding" (p. 23). When families use these new terms, they help students "build new knowledge" (p. 27) that is both meaningful and lasting.
